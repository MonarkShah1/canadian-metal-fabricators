## Division 10 Challenges

Division 10 items often come with unique challenges:

**Aesthetic Requirements:** These are visible components. Surface finish, color matching, and installation quality matter.

**Coordination with Finishes:** Items install after drywall and paint. Damage prevention and clean installation are critical.

**Institutional Standards:** Healthcare, education, and government projects have specific compliance requirements (CSA, ULC, accessibility codes).

**Last-Minute Changes:** Owner-furnished equipment and signage content often finalize late, compressing fabrication timelines.
