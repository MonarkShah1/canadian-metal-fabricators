## Ready for the End User.

Why pay to ship air? We assemble your components into finished units, kit them with hardware, and package them in your branded boxes.

### Capabilities
*   **PEM Insertion:** Automated hardware insertion for consistent torque specs.
*   **Riveting:** Structural blind rivets and solid rivets.
*   **Electrical:** Installing harnesses, grounds, and terminal blocks.
*   **Packaging:** Barcoding, labeling, and palletizing to retail requirements.

### Value Add
By letting us handle assembly, you reduce SKU complexity and free up your own labor force for higher-value tasks.
