## Strategic Sourcing.

We bypass local distributors for high-volume jobs, purchasing directly from certified mills in Korea, India, and Vietnam.

### Materials We Stock
*   **Steel:** HRPO, CR, Galvanized (G90/G60), Satin Coat.
*   **Stainless:** 304, 316, 430 (Food & Medical Grade).
*   **Aluminum:** 5052, 6061, 3003.
*   **Copper & Brass:** Bus bars and decorative finishes.

### Traceability
Every coil and sheet is tracked by Heat Number. Full MTRs are provided with every shipment, ensuring compliance with your quality standards.
